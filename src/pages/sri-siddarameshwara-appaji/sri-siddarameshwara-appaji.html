Life Sketch of Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwara Appaji

The form of Guru is the root of meditation. The feet of Guru are the root of worship. The teaching of the Guru is the root of all Mantras. The Grace of Guru is the root of Salvation.

Param Pujya Shri Appaji Crores of Prostration to Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameswara Appaji who is the Source of Absolute Consciousness-Bliss, who is the Essence of Truth and Bliss. Due to whose Existence the mugalkhod Sampradaya upheld. Due to his Bliss all are happy, who is the Source of Happiness, who is always pleased, who is the Essence of Knowledge, who is identical with the Real Existence.

I bow to that Guru Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameswara Appaji, the dispeller of avidya, the destroyer of all obstacles, one who drives away the six enemies from my vicinity they are Kama (lust), Krodha (anger), Moha (excessive attachment or delusion), Lobha (greed), Mada (pride), Matsarya (jealousy). Even a droplet of water from Appaji's lotus feet can cure grave illness, all miseries will come to an end. Let my attachment to the lotus feet of the Lord never diminish.

It is believed that Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameswara Appaji is the reincarnation of Sri sri Siddalinga Appaji.

Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameswara Appaji is considered to be magnanimous and benevolent, countless devotees of his testify to the miracles he has performed. He was a kind, merciful guru who is always forthcoming in our times of need.

His glories are boundless; yet His Holiness is very simple, humble and completely free from egotism. Thousands visit His abode every day to have His darshana and return as the recipients of immense grace. A compassionate glance from His most merciful eyes rends asunder even the greatest impediment to a joyful life.

He is still performing many miracles, It is said Deserving Blind get their Sight, Deserving Deaf and Dumb hear and Speak, fulfills all the righteous desires, all evils, malefic effects of black magic, diseases vanish by saying "OM SHRI SHADAKSHARI SHIVAYOGI SIDDARAMESHWARAYANAMHA" with devotion and by pleasing Sadguru Siddaram.

Millions of his followers, disciples and devotees have expressed that the mere thought of him and meditation with the repetition of the following mantra would address any difficulties that they have in dealing with their day to day problems.

" SRI RAM SIDDARAM SIDDARAM JAYA RAM

SIDDARAM SIDDARAM RAM RAM SIDDARAM "

Birth and childhood... Born to smt. Sharanamma and Sri Channaveerayya, on Sept. 18, 1928 at Jambagi village, Aland Tq, Gulbarga district. he was named Siddarameshwar. His ancestors were of the Jangama community (Hindu).

It is said that Sri Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji visited their house and professed that this boy Siddaram is not an ordinary child & he will be a great saint and show light of spiritualism to people living in ignorance.

Early Childhood and miracles

At the tender years of three and four, he use to rehearse the verses of Bhajan-Kirtan at home, what he has learned at various sacred places he visited along with his mother.

The child became the pet of the entire village His playmates called him, Guru and he was called Brahmajnani, a "Realized Soul", by the neighbors, as he comforted friends in distress and never seemed to get cross or tired.

He never went to school properly, several times he told his mother that "I know all these, and my school is completely different from these and there is no such school which I want to go."

When he was around 10 years old, he noticed that an eight year old boy about to die from poisonous snake bite. Sri balayogi Siddaram told grief stricken parents not to cry and apply prasada (Bhasma) to his forehead by remembering their family Deity, she did so, as told by balayogi the young boy woke up as though he was getting up from a sleep. The joy of the ecstatic parents knew no bounds.

He increased the time for mediation and realized that God alone is one reality & essence of life. His concentration increased greatly due to regular, pure meditation and unknowingly all siddhis (mystical powers) attended upon him at the tender age of ten years. No one could stop the fire of renunciation that was enveloping him completely.

Entry into asceticism, Penance(Tapassya) and Peethadhipati of Achaleri Mutt:

A Guru from Jiddagee once came to Jambaga village and told Siddaram parents this boy is born to renounce the world, he convinced his parents and took Siddaram to Jiddagee mutt.

Pujya Appaji choosen as Mutthadhipati of Achaleri mutt by Jiddagee Guru on june 05 1960. This mutt is in Achaleri village, tq:Umarga, Dt:Usmanabad, Maharashtra state.

It is one of historical place nearly 28 Jeeva Samadhi's found at this place. It is said that lord Shiva himself appeared before one of his devotees at this place.

Pujya Appaji's Daily routine was to go rounds for collecting biksha (alms), some people use to give and some others used to tease. A description of the hardships faced by him will melt anybodyâ€™s heart.There are some days he never went for collecting alms, and remained meditating without food and water for months. He was a great saint in the real sense, as he led a simple life without any worldly desires inspite of his exalted position at the prestigious Achaleri Mutt.

Chosen Successor (Uttaradhikari) :

Sri Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji and Sri Sri Kashinath Maharaj decided to choose Sri Balayogi Siddarameshwar as his successor; necessary arrangements were made on 1st June 1964 named him as successor to mugalkhod and all other Mutts. The great Guru hovered Purna Guru Kripa on him. Sri Yallaling Appaji blessed Siddrameshwar with ultimate knowledge, thereafter Siddarameshwar was transformed into Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwar Maharaj by the golden touch of SadGurudev (master). Siddarameshwar got enlightenment.

The end of sadhana was reached at last. The human being became a divine being. Each moment of life was overflowing with the divine realization of existence of One in All.

The stage beyond pride & material attractions was reached. The final destination where the Jiva becomes one with Parmatman was reached. The blissful state where nothing can separate one from God was finally attained.

Pilgrimages:

It is one of the injunctions of Sanyasis to move constantly and spread the gospel of truth, greatness of God and duties of human beings to God and Community. So travelling was a must. Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwara Appaji also undertook a tour. Soon after becoming the Sadguru, he began a series of piligrimages that took him to several places. he covered a vast area geographically and started establishing mutts where devotees and disciples had desired to have one.

Wherever he went showered his blessings to the people who visited him.

Historic Summit at Kakkalameli:

At the birth place of Sharano Shankara Siddaling Maharaj, Pujya Appaji organised a historic summit of 63 Jagadgurus who had renounced the world with their spiritual knowledge. It was a long cherished desire of Pujya Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji. This desire was fulfilled by Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwar Appaji on Feb. 23 1998. This place kakkalameli was severly hit by drought when the summit took place, with Appaji blessings this summit was a grand success nearly 5 to 10 lakhs of devotees attended this summit.

Pujya Appaji's admiration :

Those who have had the great fortune of seeing both the Appaji together could have noticed the great reverence with which Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwara conducted himself in the presence of his Guru. Pujya Appaji used to visit Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji every morning, offer His prostrations at the most sacred lotus feet of His beloved Guru and only then commence the dayâ€™s work. Whenever Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji spoke to Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwara Appaji, the latter would reverentially bend forward and listen. Whenever They walked together, Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwar Appaji would never over-step his Guru. Whenever both of Them were to occupy their seats, Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwar Appaji would wait for Sri Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji to be seated first. Sometimes when Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwar Appaji arrived a little later than Sri Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji to attend a function, He would pay his respects to His Guru first and only then take his seat. Sri Sri Shadakshari Shivayogi Siddarameshwar Appaji would listen to Sri Sri Sadguru Yallaling Appaji's discourses intently.

Entry into Jidga(Sadhana Kshetra) Anand Ashram and NavaKalyan Mutt :

Pujya appaji arrived at his sadhana kshetra Jidga, for realization of ultimate truth in 1982.

He built a Mutt with the help of his disciples, and his devotees named it as Anubhava Ashram and NavaKalyana Mutt, true to its name whoever visit these mutt and pray for his benevolence will get more than what he/she expected.
